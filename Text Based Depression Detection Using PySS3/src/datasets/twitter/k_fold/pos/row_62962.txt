The story of the wishing well!  

Sam: "I'm Sam?"

The Wishing well: "Call me Aristotle."

Sam: "How did you get that name?"

The Wishing well: "I didn't say that that's my name! I just said you can call me that if you want that!"

Sam: *Throws a penny*

Sam: "I wish..."

The Wishing well: "I want that the grass here was greener!"

Sam: "Hey, why do you wish something? I threw the penny!"

The Wishing well: "Who here is the wishing well me or you?"
