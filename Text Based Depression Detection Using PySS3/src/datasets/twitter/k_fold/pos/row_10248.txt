Here's some great dietary advice! One day, you'll want to get into good shape. But all the diets won't work, and no matter what you do won't work. Try [carb cycling](https://www.healthline.com/nutrition/carb-cycling-101)! It's a diet that focuses on cycling through high-carb foods and low-carb foods throughout the week (hence the name). To summarise it from the details of the link above,

&gt; The proposed mechanism of carb cycling is to maximize the benefits of carbohydrates and teach the body to burn fat as fuel.

It's also stated from the link that "while this makes sense in theory, more direct research is needed." Go and read the rest of it for more details!
