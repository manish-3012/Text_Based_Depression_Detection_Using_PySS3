Worth the waitSometimes I wonder why people allow themselves to live on when life keeps smacking them in the face. It always seems to be for me the unknowing of what tomorrow holds, and how quickly everything can change. I have wanted for a long time to be at peace and I feel I won't find it in this life, however it is to precious to waiste. There is a great relief when you no longer fear death and go into everyday seeking opportunity. Remember that and that going into the unknown is the same alive or dead
