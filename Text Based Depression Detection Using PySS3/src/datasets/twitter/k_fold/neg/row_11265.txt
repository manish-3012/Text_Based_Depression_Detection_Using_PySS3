The long battleThe battle began many years ago. 

It started out small and began to grow. 

Distractions like work could only slow,

The sadness inside of wanting to go. 

He added more responsibility. 

He even added a family. 

It'll get better he told himself. 

And he put his concerns upon a shelf.

Then one day that shelf came crashing down. 

No longer could he stop the frown.

And he felt as if he could drown. 

Stay! They begged; they pleaded and begged. 

So he set out to rebuild that silly shelf. 

But the pieces were scattered all over the floor and the items were piled larger than before...
