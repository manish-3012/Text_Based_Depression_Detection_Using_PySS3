Why Suicide Is Not the SolutionSomeone dear in my life committed suicide two years ago. If I had known he was struggling, I would have done anything to help, and I sincerely mean that. I want to speak to those who are currently fighting with suicidal thoughts. Please read this [article](https://medium.com/@sarahksara/the-why-nots-of-suicide-3d8be0582a8f). My mother spent about three weeks carefully crafting it with much research and editing, and I believe it could really change your perspective.
