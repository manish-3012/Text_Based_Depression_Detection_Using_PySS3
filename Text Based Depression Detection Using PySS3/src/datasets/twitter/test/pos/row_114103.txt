Elemental4, an element-combination game with a voting system!  I just found this neat game online. You know how there's those free app games that allow you to combine the 4 basic elements of nature to get new items and fill out your collection? This website does that, except you can VOTE on new elements that get added into the game! I've been playing it for a while, and it's quite the entertaining trip. Try it!
