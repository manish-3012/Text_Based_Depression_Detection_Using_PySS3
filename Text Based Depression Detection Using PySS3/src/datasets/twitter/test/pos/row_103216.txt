To anyone who looks in the mirror and doesn't like the face they see: Stop looking in one of those funky fun house mirrors and get a real one.

You are all beautiful. It doesn't matter if you're underweight, overweight, have bad acne, glasses or braces. If you're tall or short or have six fingers. You are all amazing people so never say you aren't. I'm actually begging you. We need more optimism in this world and it should start we you guys.
