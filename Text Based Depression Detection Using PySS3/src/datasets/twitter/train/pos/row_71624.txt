Tip on how to be a good friend Text them. Even text them first sometimes. Don't always wait for them to reach out to you. Because that makes it look like you don't care. Be the best friend you would want them to be for you. In order to have good friends you have to be one. Send them good morning texts. Tell them goodnight. Occasionally check up on them. Ask them how're they're doing. Make it clear you care about them and that they're somewhat important to you. If it's a friendship you value, act like it.
