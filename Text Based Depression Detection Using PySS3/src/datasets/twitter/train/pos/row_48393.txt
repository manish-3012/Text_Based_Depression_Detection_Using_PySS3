Daily tribute to heroes and loyalists of the imperium: day 1, Rylanor the Ancient Of Rites Rylanor, known as Ancient Rylanor and Rylanor the Unyielding, was a [Venerable Dreadnought](https://warhammer40k.fandom.com/wiki/Venerable_Dreadnought) and hero of the [Emperor's Children](https://warhammer40k.fandom.com/wiki/Emperor%27s_Children) [Legion](https://warhammer40k.fandom.com/wiki/Legion) during the [Great Crusade](https://warhammer40k.fandom.com/wiki/Great_Crusade). He served as the IIIrd Legion's "Ancient of Rites," and had been mortally wounded battling the [Aeldari](https://warhammer40k.fandom.com/wiki/Aeldari) in the late 30th Millennium, solar decades before the outbreak of the [Horus Heresy](https://warhammer40k.fandom.com/wiki/Horus_Heresy). Following his wounding, Rylanor was interred within the [adamantium](https://warhammer40k.fandom.com/wiki/Adamantium) shell of a [Contemptor Pattern Dreadnought](https://warhammer40k.fandom.com/wiki/Contemptor_Pattern_Dreadnought).
