Pro Tip for Among Us When 2 colors are calling each other out and one of them swears its the other color, tell them: "[Insert Color Name], Can we vote you next round if its not [Color they say is the imposter]?"

An imposter will Never say yes to this question. 
Example:
Red(Imposter): Its has to be blue, I saw him vent in Security.
Blue: Nah its not me. 
You: Red, can we vote you next round if its not blue?
Red: Uhh....
