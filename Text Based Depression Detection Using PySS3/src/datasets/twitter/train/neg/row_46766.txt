Reasons why no one should commit suicide.Someone has to find your body. 
And then that person has to live with that. Whether he or she knew you or not, it will affect them. Whether they've seen a dead body every day of their life or not, it will affect them.

Someone is going to lose you.
And then that person, or people most likely, have to live without you. No matter what you tell yourself, no one is better off without you.

I tell myself this every day and it's kept me alive for years.
