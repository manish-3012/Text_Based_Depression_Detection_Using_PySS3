Why.We all know the stereotype of the little kid that won't stop asking "why," and everyone is annoyed at that little kid. Well I challenge that. Keep asking why. Ask why until the answer is "I don't know," and then ask it again. Why do we work? For money. Why? So we can buy food. Why? So we can survive. Why? So we can reproduce. Why? So we can continue the bloodline. Why? Because it's how we're hardwired as a species. Why? ...




I apply this to life. My counselor talks about how I should want to feel happy. Why? Happiness is a chemical reaction. Totally insignificant. It means nothing. If the universe was vaporized right now, your happiness would mean nothing. And neither would the existence of the universe. Oh, what, the universe DOES have importance? Why?
